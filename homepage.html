Comprehensive Healthcare Services

Our range of services is comprehensive, covering everything from routine check-ups and preventive care to advanced diagnostics and complex treatments. Vivy's Medical Care is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, allowing us to offer the latest in medical advancements. Our services include, but are not limited to:

Primary Care: Routine examinations, immunizations, and health screenings.

Specialist Consultations: Access to specialists in cardiology, neurology, oncology, and more.

Diagnostic Services: Advanced imaging, lab tests, and other diagnostic procedures.

Chronic Disease Management: Tailored care plans for conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma.

Pediatrics: Comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents.

A Seamless Booking Experience

Understanding that time is of the essence, Vivy's Medical Care has streamlined the appointment scheduling process to be as efficient and user-friendly as possible. Patients can easily book appointments through our intuitive online portal, which offers real-time availability and instant confirmations. For those who prefer a more personal touch, our friendly staff is always ready to assist with scheduling over the phone. We also offer telehealth services, allowing patients to consult with their doctors from the comfort of their homes.
